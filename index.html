The Journey

At 23, my life is anchored by my faith in God, my marriage, and a daily commitment to growth. I am a Christian and a husband preparing to step back into the amateur boxing ring after years away. I don’t claim to have it all figured out, but I have a relentless commitment to showing up, fighting to improve, and documenting the reality of the grind—with the ultimate goal of challenging and encouraging young men to step up and do the same.

My Pillars

Faith as the Foundation

Everything built on sand will eventually fall. I anchor my ambitions, my marriage, and my daily actions in an unyielding relationship with Jesus.

Discipline Over Motivation

Motivation is a fleeting feeling; discipline is a daily choice. True progress is forged in the dark mornings when you don’t feel like showing up, but you execute anyway.

Character Over Reputation

Reputation is merely what others think of you; character is who you are when nobody is watching. I focus on the depth of my character, not the applause of the crowd.

Growth Through Sacrifice

Comfort is the enemy of excellence. To reach the next level, you must be willing to sacrifice your old habits, your comfort zones, and the things holding you back. We fight to improve daily, setting a standard for the next generation of young men to follow.

Building A Legacy

"A life built with purpose extends far beyond yourself. My vision for the future isn't just about personal achievements—it’s about establishing a lasting legacy of strength, faith, and leadership for my family, honoring the commitments we make today for generations to come."

Keynote Speaking

Bringing raw perspective, high-intensity principles, and practical strategies to youth events, sports clubs, and faith-based men's gatherings.

1. Forging Relentless Discipline

How to break dependence on temporary emotion and build a bulletproof daily routine. This presentation delivers actionable frameworks on showing up when you don't feel like it, conquering the early morning, and mastering execution.

2. Fighting to Step Up: The Call to Young Men

A direct, high-conviction talk challenging the culture of passivity and comfort. Focusing on character over reputation, taking extreme ownership of your life, and building a firm foundation of faith and leadership.

3. The Mindset of the Ring

Drawing raw parallels from boxing training to life's ultimate grinds. Learning how to take a hit, adapt under intense pressure, control the pace of your environment, and stay locked onto your God-given purpose.
